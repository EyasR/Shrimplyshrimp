Home Information

At Shrimply Shrimp, we specialize in breeding high-quality freshwater shrimp with a focus on health, color, and consistency. Every shrimp is carefully raised in stable, well-maintained tanks to ensure strong genetics and vibrant appearance.

We are passionate about the shrimp hobby and committed to providing hobbyists and collectors with high grade good looking shrimp. Whether you're just starting out or expanding your collection, our goal is to deliver shrimp that thrive in your aquarium.

Our Process

Each shrimp is bred and raised in controlled environments with consistent water parameters, proper nutrition, and regular monitoring. We take the time to ensure quality over quantity. Shipping & Care We use proven packing methods to keep shrimp safe during transit. Orders are shipped with insulation and care to maintain temperature and water quality. Our mission to provide reliable, high-quality shrimp while helping grow the shrimp-keeping community through transparency and care.

FAQ

We recommend feeding your shrimp Shrimp Dinner 2, Hikari Shrimp Cuisine, Brightwell FlorinBacter, and MicroBacter7.

The Black Boa is a highly sought-after premium metallic variant of Caridina shrimp. They are known for their deep black body coloration, paired with a striking metallic silver-to-gold back stripe.

Low/Medium Grade: Mostly black with a thinner, less-defined metallic stripe.

High Grade: Features a wide, solid, and unbroken metallic stripe that extends from the head to the tail.

We strongly advise against using tap water. Its parameters can fluctuate significantly and it often contains minerals that raise KH levels. We highly recommend using RO/DI or purified water, remineralized with a dedicated GH+ product such as SaltyShrimp Bee Shrimp Mineral GH+.
